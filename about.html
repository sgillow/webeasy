About Computer Science

CS is the study of computational systems, primarily dealing with the design and development of software. A common misconception is that computer science involves solely programming; in reality, it involves algorithmic thinking in order to solve problems, whether those problems are as simple as creating a website, to maintaining a secure database. Programming is one of many tools computer scientists have in their arsenal.
